Kelvin K Lok. Designer. Manager.

Presently the Experience Strategy Lead for Mastercard Commercial Solutions, where I help shape the future of our growing design organization.

I have the pleasure of collaborating with talented people where my knack for digital interaction, user experience, creativity, and technology help us deliver innovative and scalable value to our customers.

As an evangelist for design thinking and research practices, I thrive in teams where I can challenge my skills to design accessible, responsive, globally deployed products, recruit and mentor designers, and develop strategies to minimize failure while empowering innovation.
